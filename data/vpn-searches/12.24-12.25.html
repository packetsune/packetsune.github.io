This data was sourced from Google Trends on the 11th of December 2025

This site shows the interest score(?) on Google Trends for VPN-related topics broken down by territory. (Most of these territories are generally recognized as countries, however certain subregions such as Hong Kong are separate, often due to significant regulatory differences.)
